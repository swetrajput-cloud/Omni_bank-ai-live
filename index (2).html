Executive Business Summary
Deployed across a 500-agent contact centre processing 50,000+ interactions per month, OmniBank AI is projected to deliver an annual cost reduction of ₹12 crore through intelligent automation of average handle time, first-contact resolution uplift, and proactive complaint triage.
Regulatory Alignment
Fully compliant with RBI's 30-day grievance resolution directive. SLA breach rate reduced from 23% to 2.1% via predictive breach alerting.
Customer Retention Impact
Churn risk scoring and real-time sentiment triage enable proactive outreach, reducing high-value customer attrition by an estimated 34%.
Scalability & Integration
API-first architecture integrates with core banking (Finacle/Temenos), Salesforce CRM, and existing telephony (Genesys/Avaya) with zero downtime deployment.
Estimated Payback Period
Platform implementation cost recovered within 4–6 months based on AHT savings alone, with CSAT and retention benefits accruing from day one.
